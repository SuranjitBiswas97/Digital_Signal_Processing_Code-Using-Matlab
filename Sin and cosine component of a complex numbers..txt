Task 2
Sin and cosine component of a complex numbers.

%Lab 5
close all
clear
clc
j=sqrt(-1);

f=1;
w=2*pi*f;
fs=100;
t=0:1/fs:5;
y=exp(j*w*t);
figure
plot(t,real(y));
title('real Part of complex exponential variable e^j^w^t');
xlabel('t');
ylabel('Real(e^j^w^t)');
figure;
plot(t,imag(y));
title('imaginary Part of complex exponential variable e^j^w^t');
xlabel('t');
ylabel('Imaginary(e^j^w^t)');
